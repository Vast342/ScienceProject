Air Quality Conundrum | Joseph Pasfield

Controls:
↑ / W / Spacebar: Jump
← / A: Move Left
↓ / S: Dive
→ / D: Move Right

The year is 2050, humanity is on the brink of collapse due to death by air quality. As a slime instead of a human, you are chosen to go to the worst areas and decrease pollution by any means necessary.
You can roll up walls and drop down fast by deforming your body to change velocity! However, you can only pick up objects one at a time!
