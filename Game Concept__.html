Game Concept

Air Quality Conundrum

Description

The year is 2050, humanity is on the brink of collapse due to death by air quality. As a slime instead of a human, you are chosen to go to the worst areas and decrease pollution by any means necessary. Complete all 3 tasks to save the world!

Instructions:

  • Use the W and D keys to move side to side, and use the spacebar to jump!
  • Touch items to pick them up! As a slime you can only carry 1 item at a time!
  • Move to the Item repository to place the item!
  • Move all items to repositories to complete the minigame!
